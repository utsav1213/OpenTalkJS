Here's a sample resignation letter template:

[Your Name]
[Your Address]
[City, State ZIP Code]
[Date]

[Recipient's Name]
[Recipient's Title]
[Company/Organization Name]
[Company/Organization Address]
[City, State ZIP Code]

Dear [Recipient's Name],

Please accept this letter as formal notice of my resignation from my position as [Your Position] at [Company/Organization Name], effective [Date of Last Day of Work].

I appreciate the opportunities I have had while working at [Company/Organization Name], and I am grateful for the experience and knowledge gained during my time here. However, I have decided that it is time for me to move on and explore new challenges.

I will do everything possible to ensure a smooth transition of my responsibilities and complete any outstanding tasks before my departure. If there is anything specific you would like me to focus on during my remaining time here, please let me know.

Thank you again for the opportunity to work at [Company/Organization Name]. I wish the organization continued success in the future.

Sincerely,

[Your Signature]
[Your Name]

**Optional Sections:**

* If you're leaving for a new job, you can include a statement about your enthusiasm for the new role and when you'll start.
Example: "I am excited to begin my new role as [New Position] at [New Company/Organization] on [Date of Start]."
* If you have any outstanding vacation time or other benefits, you may want to mention them separately.
Example: "I will be taking [number] days of paid vacation time from [Date] to [Date]."
* If there's anything you'd like to leave on record for future reference, now is the time.

**Tips:**

* Keep your resignation letter professional and concise.
* Avoid negative comments about the company or colleagues.
* Proofread carefully before submitting.
* Consider keeping a copy of the letter for your records.
